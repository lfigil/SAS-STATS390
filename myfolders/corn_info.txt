Corn Yields and Rainfall DATA DESCRIPTION:

There are three variables:

year: from 1909 to 1927 

yield: yearly corn yield in bushels per acre, in six Corn Belt
 states(Iowa, Illinois, Nebraska, Missouri, Indiana, and
 Ohio).

rain: rainfall measurements in inches, in the six states.


SOURCE:
 M.Ezekiel and K.A.Fox, Methods of Correlation and
 Regression Analysis, p.212.Copyright 1959, John Wiley
 and Sons, Inc., New York.   

Data originally from E. G.
 Misner, "Studies of the Relationship of Weather to the
 Production and Price of Farm Products, I. Corn",
 mimeographed publication, Cornell University, March 1928.
